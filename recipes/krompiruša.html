Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour (350g), plus more for dusting & kneading
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp salt (3g)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil (50ml)
  • 2/3 cup water (150ml)
  • 3-4 large potatoes, or 6-7 small ones, peeled and diced into very small chunks
  • 2 onions, diced into small chunks
  • salt & pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Mixe the flour
  2. Knead for 23 minutes
  3. Cut potatoes
  4. Roll out the dough
  5. Devide the dough into 4-5 small balls
  6. Spoon the potato into a line
  7. Place the dough into the pan
  8. Bake at 175 deg. Celsius
